WHAT

atelier KARLA aK ?

atelierKARLA offers a new approach to branding and space design. If you have an idea but it is not sufficiently developed, contact me and together we can make it happen! Through the concepts and of manual sketches, brainstorming and various ideas related to project management, we can design your perfect space in which you will carry on with your job or everyday life. You will be able to see initial interior sketches and plans, contact us for renovation or projects of small houses and offices. we also do urban site planning and analysis (as large scale projects), as well as branding and logo design.
